About Us

5thJohnson is a women's fashion brand and retailer founded in 2018. Our mission is to craft and curate hyperfeminine, seductive pieces that make women feel confident. We draw inspiration from silky fabrics, delicate florals and vintage pieces to dress the modern woman and traditional Africa.

We consciously partner with Nigerian manufacturers and brand partners, fostering the country's fashion industry and creating jobs.

We offer timeless pieces crafted from high-quality materials that are designed to last beyond fleeting trends.

We understand that looking good shouldn't damage the environment, which is why 80% of our packaging is biodegradable, minimizing our environmental footprint.

Thank you for supporting us
